What is NIOSH

The NIOSH method is a guide used to assess and improve manual material handling processes.This method,incorporating ergonomic principles,evaluates factors such as the weight of the load,lifting distance,lifting speed,carrying distance,and the worker's posture.It aims to enable workers to safely lift and carry heavy loads,providing recommendations to reduce risk in manual material handling task.
